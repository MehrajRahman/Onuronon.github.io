About Onuronon

Onuronon is a Youth development Organisation of Bangladesh. Our main pursuance is to help the youth of our country for developing themselves and patronize our country a skillful youth.

Onuronon,it means resonance which comes from the inner skill of a youth.The idea of forming 'Onuronon' first came into the mind of 'Mahmudur Rahman Mehraj'. He is the founder and president of our organization.
Currently we are working on engaging people who are capable enough to be a part of this organisation.Onuronon always follow the term 'chain of command'. That's why everything is well planned and organized. Our executive team is full of dedicated and skilled member who are ready to go any further for the betterment of the youth. Since Onuronon is a Youth empowerment and delelopment organization ,we are planning to provide every kind of amenities to the youth for empowering themselves. Onuronon is going to organize several Online event . There will be numorous number of competition .The main purpose of those events is to find talented and condign youth and train them afterward. We have a rich number of skilled instructor who will train and guide the youth . We are also going to launch several online paid courses on different online platforms. Everyone can increase their skills by following those courses. This 21st century is mainly dependent on internet. We will arrange course on programming,coding,blogging,public speaking,photography,editing and many more. Our goal is to prepare the youth to meet the challenges of 21st century.

Onuronon, A youth organisation works for building a skilled generations.
